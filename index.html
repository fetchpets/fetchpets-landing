FETCHPETS™

Helping pets find people — faster, safer, better.

FetchPets™ is a mobile application designed to help people discover adoptable pets from verified shelters and rescues. Browse detailed pet profiles, explore compatibility insights, and start the adoption journey directly with shelters.

FetchPets works directly with participating shelters and rescue organizations to help streamline the pet adoption process.

Why FetchPets Exists

Millions of adoptable pets are waiting for homes, while shelters face overcrowding and limited visibility.

  • Making pet discovery simple and engaging
  • Giving shelters modern tools to present adoptable animals
  • Helping adopters make thoughtful, informed connections

No endless searching. No outdated listings. Just clearer paths to adoption.

How It Works

1. Discover Pets

Browse adoptable pets from verified shelters and rescue organizations.

2. Personalized Discovery

View helpful details such as age, size, energy level, and temperament.

3. Connect Securely

Shelters guide all screening, approvals, and next steps.

For Adopters

  • Real, adoptable pets from verified shelters
  • Clear profiles with photos and key details
  • Save favorites and track interest
  • Adoption-focused — not a marketplace

For Shelters & Rescues

  • Increased visibility for adoptable pets
  • Modern, easy-to-manage pet profiles
  • Direct engagement with potential adopters
  • No spam, resale, or brokers

FetchPets is built with shelters, not around them.

Trust & Safety First

  • Verified shelter and rescue partners
  • No pet sales or breeders
  • Privacy-first design
  • Adoption-only platform
